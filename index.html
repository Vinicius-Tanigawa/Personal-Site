Technological Development of Batteries and Its Effects on Eletrification of Aeronautics
PIBIC - CNPq

  • The main objective is to demonstrate the impacts of futuristic batteries on 100% electrical aicrafts sphere. Also the socio-environmental consequences it may cause.

  • Usage of SUAVE program, developed by Stanford University, NASA and Embraer to do the aircraft and mission's simulations. It is an open source conceptual level aircraft design environment built with the ability to analyze and optimize both conventional and unconventional designs based on Python.

  • Usage of OpenLCA program to simulate socio-environmental impacts. It is an open source and free software for Sustainability and Life Cycle Assessment.
